About

I am a writer, content creator, editor, and technical writer. The values of respect for the environment and dignity for all peoples permeate all my actions.

In my usability (UX) work, I am driven by a desire to create environments that are useable by folks from all walks of life. If each person is unique, then no one can be totally normal. Therefore, instead of creating content in format that works extremely well for one group of people, I aim to create formats that can be adjusted to each person's unique needs.

We are proud of our interiors.

Intern at Cedar Creek May 2018–Aug 2018

Cedar Creek Ecosystem Science Reserve is a University of Minnesota biological field station with many ecosystems and species found throughout the forests and grasslands of North America. Faculty, staff and students who work at Cedar Creek are dedicated to understanding how human activities, such as agriculture and fossil fuel combustion, are changing ecosystems. Many of the experiments at Cedar Creek consider the long-term consequences of human-driven environmental changes.

  • Assist researchers in collecting data and setting up long-term research projects
  • Maintain the groundbreaking “Big Bio” experiment, formally known as Biodiversity II: Effects of Plant Biodiversity on Population and Ecosystem Processes
  • Learn to identify and sort native plants
  • Create youth activities with the Education and Community Engagement department
